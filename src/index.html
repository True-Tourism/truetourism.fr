FAQ

What is True Tourism?

True Tourism is a new peer-to-peer tourism application focused on Marseille. It offers personalized and authentic tours led by passionate local guides.

How does it work?

To use True Tourism, simply download the app from the App Store or Play Store, and create an account. Then, you can browse available tours and book the one that fits your interests and schedule.
